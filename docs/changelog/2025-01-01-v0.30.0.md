Happy New Year! This release brings 2025 Wrapped, PDF archives, improved reader settings, custom avatars, and enhanced Reddit crawling.

<!-- truncate -->

## What's New

### 2025 Wrapped & Analytics

2025 Wrapped is here to celebrate your year in Karakeep! Get year-end statistics about your bookmarking activity.

### PDF Archiving

Automatically generate PDFs during crawling or on-demand. Enable this feature by setting `CRAWLER_STORE_PDF=true`.

### Unified Reader Settings

Your reader preferences (font, size, and other settings) now sync across all your devices. Device-specific overrides are also available if you prefer different settings on different devices.

### Custom User Avatars

Upload custom user avatars for more personalized profiles.

### AI Customization

- Configure tag styling including case, separators, and language on a per-user basis
- Toggle auto-tagging and auto-summarization independently

### Matter Integration

Import your libraries from Matter with full export capabilities.

## Technical Improvements

- Fixed slow bookmark queries through missing database indices
- Improved Ollama summaries using the generate endpoint
- Better YouTube thumbnail and Amazon product image extraction
- Unified Matter metadata extraction
- OpenTelemetry integration with OTLP exporter
- Node.js 24 as default (current LTS)

## Mobile & UX Enhancements

- Mobile settings screens feel more native
- OLED-friendly Android colors
- Improved iOS toast positioning
- Pending list invites show as badges

## Breaking Changes

In bookmark APIs, `includeContent` now defaults to `false` instead of `true`.
